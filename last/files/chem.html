Chemistry Topics

Start with these Easier/Introductory Chapters:

  • Periodic Table – Foundation for understanding trends across elements
  • GOC I & II
  • Chemical Bonding –hybridization and molecular shapes
  • Solutions – Raoult's law and colligative properties
  • Aldehydes, Ketones, and Acids

Move to Moderately Challenging Chapters:

  • Coordination Compounds – ligands and crystal field theory
  • Chemical Kinetics
  • Electrochemistry
  • P Block Elements – Groups 15-18 are detailed but mostly factual, requiring some memorization

Tackle the More Difficult Chapters Later:

  • Thermodynamics – enthalpy, entropy, and Gibbs energy
  • Equilibrium –Le Chatelier's principle and equilibrium constants
  • D and F Block Elements – oxidation states and properties, heavy on memorization
  • Name Reactions
  • Biomolecules
