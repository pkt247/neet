Physics Topics

Electrodynamics (30-35% weightage)

  • Electrostatics – Coulomb’s law, electric field, potential, capacitors
  • Current Electricity – Ohm's law, circuits, Kirchhoff’s laws, Wheatstone bridge
  • Magnetic Effects of Current and Magnetism – Biot-Savart law, Ampere's law, electromagnetic induction, transformers

Mechanics (15-20% weightage)

  • Laws of Motion – Newton's laws, friction, and applications
  • Work, Energy, and Power – Conservation of energy, work-energy theorem
  • Gravitation – Universal law, motion of planets, satellites

Thermodynamics and Kinetic Theory (8-10% weightage)

  • Thermodynamics – First law, second law, heat engines, Carnot engine
  • Kinetic Theory of Gases – Ideal gas equation, kinetic energy of gases

Optics and Waves (10-12% weightage)

  • Ray Optics – Reflection, refraction, lenses, mirrors, total internal reflection
  • Wave Optics – Young's double-slit experiment, interference, diffraction

Modern Physics (20-25% weightage)

  • Photoelectric Effect – Einstein’s equation, applications
  • Atoms and Nuclei – Bohr’s model, radioactive decay, nuclear reactions
  • Semiconductors – Diodes, transistors, logic gates, basic digital electronics

Key Focus Areas

  • Mechanics, Electrodynamics, and Modern Physics are the most crucial
  • Cover Optics and Thermodynamics quickly to strengthen your preparation
